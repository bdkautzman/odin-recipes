Smashburgers

Description

A remanent of my time as a line cook back in college. This smashburger is meaty, cheesy, and has just the right amount of tang from the sauce. An all time favorite!

Ingredients

  • Hamburger
  • Heirloom tomato
  • Dill pickles
  • Cheddar cheese
  • White onion
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonaise
  • Yellow mustard
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Dice onion and caramelize in a low heat pan
  2. Roll two spheres of hamburger meat
  3. Smash meat spheres into a pan at high heat
  4. After about 1 minute of cooking, flip and place cheese on the cooked side
  5. Mix ketchup, mustard, mayo and minced dill pickles
  6. Assemble burger
  7. Eat!
