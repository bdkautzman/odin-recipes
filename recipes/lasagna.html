Grandma's Lasagna

Description

One of the most delicious dishes I've ever had in my life, this recipe is straigh from my grandmother's cookbook. She was born in Italy and spent the majority of her adult life working at a restaurant in Florence. After perfecting her cooking skills, she moved back to her home town of Sivoria and opened her own cafe.

This recipe uses a traditional bechamel, rather than ricotta, and can be quite messy. I hope you will enjoy it as much as I do!

Ingredients

  • Lasanga noodles
  • San Marzano Tomatoes
  • Mozzarella
  • Fresh basil
  • Sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Dice onion
  2. Mince garlic
  3. Saute garlic and onion for 5 minutes
  4. Add tomatoes to pan, cook for an additional 15 minute
  5. Par cook lasagna
  6. Layer ingredients into the pan:
    1. Sauce
    2. Lasagna
    3. Bechamel
    4. Repeat
  7. Cook for 45 minutes at 425 degrees Fahrenheit
  8. Top with shredded Mozzarella
  9. Broil for 5 minutes
  10. Eat!
