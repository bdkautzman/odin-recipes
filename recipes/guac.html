Guac

Description

A very tasty guacamole recipe I picked up during my travels through Western Mexico.

Ingredients

  • Avocado
  • Red Onion
  • Lime
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Onion
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Dice red onion
  2. Shuck avocado and roughly chop
  3. Mash avocado, onion, garlic and salt together in a bowl
  4. Juice 1/2 lime into the bowl
  5. Stir in lime
  6. Garnish with cilantro
  7. Eat!
